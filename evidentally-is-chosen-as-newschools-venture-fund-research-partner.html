Evidentally was Chosen as a NewSchools Venture Fund Research Partner

26Feb

NewSchools Venture Fund (NewSchools) has been a supporter of Evidentally since its inception and early product stages. We have provided useful reports to over 45 of their portfolio companies to help them determine customer reach and composition; their business and feedback have helped us develop Evidence Suite in order to provide relevant information in an easy-to-read format.

Evidentally was honored to yet again be a highlighted research partner for their latest NewSchools Ignite challenge: Expanded Definition of Student Success. We provided Demographic Profiles, analyses of customer demographics, for 11 ventures who are building innovative edtech products designed to “increase equity and improve academic outcomes by supporting the development of social-emotional competencies, as well as improvements in school culture and climate.” *

For several qualifying customers, we also included additional analyses of usage patterns, geographic distribution of active users, and an assessment of the feasibility of an ESSA-aligned impact evaluation, as part of a Footprint Report.

Additionally, Evidentally has assisted NewSchools by tracking their portfolio across the different challenges by providing summary reports of ventures’ Demographic Profiles.

These reports have been well received by NewSchools in gleaning the kind of information companies need to stay on mission...economically, through products and services, and through social impact.

* https://www.newschools.org/ignite/program/expanded-definition-of-student-success-challenge/
