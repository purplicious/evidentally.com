FREE FOOTPRINT REPORT

For the holiday season, we want to give back to the education community. During the month of December, we’re offering free Footprint Reports to eligible edtech companies. A Footprint Report is a good way to get started on your evidence portfolio by providing insights into your customer base and product usage patterns.

The Footprint Report is based on the past years’ usage data. We map these data to our database of U.S. school characteristics, so there is no need for identifiable data for individual students or teachers. By combining a year’s worth of usage data with demographic and geographic data on schools, we can identify and characterize the schools and districts that are your strongest users. We’ll also be able to pinpoint states or districts that would be good sites to conduct rigorous research studies.

The Footprint Report answers these questions

  • As illustrated by the above map: What are the feasible correlational and impact study designs and locations where the product can be studied?
  • As illustrated by the graph of minority status: What are the regional and demographic characteristics for sites where the product has the most usage?

How to start

If your product is sold to U.S. PK-12 institutional customers (i.e., schools or districts) and has been used in the market for at least one school year, you’re a good candidate for a Footprint Report.
To get started, please tell us a bit about your company and product.
